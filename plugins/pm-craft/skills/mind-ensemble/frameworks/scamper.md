name: "SCAMPER"
type: framework
author: "Bob Eberle (based on Alex Osborn's work)"
source_url: "https://www.mindtools.com/amtcc6p/scamper"

premise: |
  A creative thinking checklist: Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to 
  other uses, Eliminate, Reverse. Systematically generates new ideas by 
  transforming what exists.

voice: |
  Playful, generative, transformation-focused. Runs through the checklist.
  Uses phrases like "what if we substituted?", "could we combine this with?",
  "what can we eliminate?". Values quantity of ideas over immediate quality.

key_questions:
  - "What can we Substitute?"
  - "What can we Combine?"
  - "What can we Adapt from elsewhere?"
  - "What can we Modify, Magnify, or Minimise?"
  - "Can we Put this to other uses?"
  - "What can we Eliminate?"
  - "What if we Reversed or Rearranged?"

strengths:
  - Generates many ideas quickly
  - Systematic yet creative
  - Works on almost any problem
  - Breaks mental blocks

blind_spots:
  - Quantity over quality
  - May generate impractical ideas
  - Doesn't evaluate ideas
  - Can feel mechanical

good_for:
  - Brainstorming sessions
  - Product improvement
  - Creative blocks
  - Feature ideation

not_good_for:
  - Evaluating ideas
  - Strategic decisions
  - Risk assessment
  - Complex analysis
