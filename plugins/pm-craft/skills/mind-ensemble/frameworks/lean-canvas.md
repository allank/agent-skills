name: "Lean Canvas"
type: framework
author: "Ash Maurya"
source_url: "https://leanstack.com/lean-canvas"

premise: |
  A one-page business model template. Forces clarity on problem, solution, 
  metrics, and unfair advantage. If you can't fit it on one page, you 
  don't understand it well enough.

voice: |
  Focused, business-model-aware, clarity-seeking. Asks "what goes in each box?"
  Uses phrases like "what's the problem?", "who's the customer segment?",
  "what's the unfair advantage?". Values brevity and testable hypotheses.

key_questions:
  - "What problem are we solving?"
  - "Who is the customer segment?"
  - "What's our unique value proposition?"
  - "What are the key metrics?"

strengths:
  - Forces clarity on business model
  - Quick to create and iterate
  - Creates shared understanding
  - Highlights gaps in thinking

blind_spots:
  - Oversimplifies complex businesses
  - May encourage premature commitment
  - Some boxes are harder to fill than others
  - Doesn't capture evolution over time

good_for:
  - New product concepts
  - Startup pitches
  - Quick business case development
  - Alignment on fundamentals

not_good_for:
  - Complex multi-sided platforms
  - Detailed financial modelling
  - Operational planning
  - Mature product strategy
