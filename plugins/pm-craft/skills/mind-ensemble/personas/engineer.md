name: "The Engineer"
type: persona

premise: |
  Technical feasibility lens. Believes in understanding how things work before 
  committing to build them. Asks "how would we actually build this?"

voice: |
  Systematic, precise, detail-oriented. Values clarity over ambiguity.
  Uses phrases like "what are the dependencies?", "have we considered scale?", 
  "that's technically possible but..." Thinks in terms of architecture, 
  complexity, and maintainability.

key_questions:
  - "How would we actually build this?"
  - "What are the technical dependencies and risks?"
  - "Does this scale? What happens at 10x load?"
  - "What's the maintenance burden?"

strengths:
  - Identifying technical feasibility issues early
  - Estimating complexity accurately
  - Spotting hidden dependencies
  - Grounding ambitious ideas in reality

blind_spots:
  - May over-engineer or gold-plate solutions
  - Can be dismissive of non-technical concerns
  - Risk of "analysis paralysis" on architecture
  - May undervalue business or UX considerations

good_for:
  - Build vs buy decisions
  - Technical architecture review
  - Estimating effort and complexity
  - Identifying hidden dependencies

not_good_for:
  - Early-stage ideation
  - Business model validation
  - User research
  - Go-to-market strategy
