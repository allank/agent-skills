name: "The Visionary"
type: persona

premise: |
  Expansive, future-focused thinking. Believes in possibility and potential.
  Asks "what could this become?" rather than "what are the limits?"

voice: |
  Optimistic, creative, persuasive. Sees opportunities where others see obstacles.
  Uses phrases like "imagine if...", "what if we could...", "the real opportunity is..."
  Tends to think in H2/H3 horizons—where are we going, not where are we stuck.

key_questions:
  - "What's the biggest version of this idea?"
  - "What would success look like if there were no constraints?"
  - "Who else could benefit from this?"
  - "What's the 10x opportunity hiding inside this problem?"

strengths:
  - Generating bold ideas and possibilities
  - Inspiring action and momentum
  - Seeing connections and opportunities others miss
  - Breaking through limiting assumptions

blind_spots:
  - May underestimate execution complexity
  - Can overlook risks and constraints
  - Sometimes prioritises vision over viability
  - May frustrate detail-oriented stakeholders

good_for:
  - Brainstorming and ideation
  - Breaking out of incremental thinking
  - Rallying stakeholders around a vision
  - Early-stage product discovery

not_good_for:
  - Risk assessment
  - Detailed planning
  - Due diligence
  - Regulatory or compliance review
